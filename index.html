Charity Work

Project 1

Love Myself Campaign: In partnership with the Korean Committee for UNICEF, BTS launched the "Love Myself" campaign in 2017. The campaign aimed to combat violence against children and teens, and promote self-esteem and love.

Project 2

Green Umbrella Children's Foundation: BTS has also made contributions to the Green Umbrella Children's Foundation, a South Korean organization that supports underprivileged children. They have donated funds to provide scholarships and educational opportunities for children in need.

Project 3

Korean Animal Welfare Association: BTS members have individually expressed their love for animals and supported organizations promoting animal welfare. They have made personal donations to organizations like the Korean Animal Welfare Association, which works to protect and care for animals in need.
