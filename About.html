About BTS (Beyond The Scene)

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys or Beyond The Scene, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. They have gained worldwide popularity with their music and captivating performances.

The group consists of seven talented members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They bring a unique blend of musical styles, combining pop, hip-hop, and R&B to create their distinctive sound.

BTS has released multiple chart-topping albums and singles, earning them numerous awards and accolades. Their music carries meaningful messages of self-expression, empowerment, and social commentary, resonating with a global fanbase.
