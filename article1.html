Article 1 BTS V x Celine

Published on: May 15, 2022

Author: Charlie

BTS member Kim Taehyung, also known as V, recently sat down with ELLE Korea for an interview and shared his thoughts on his love for the fashion brand, CELINE. The singer, who is known for his impeccable fashion sense, was given the title of “Celine Boy” by the magazine.

It's clear that Taehyung has invested a lot of time and effort into nurturing his relationship with CELINE and Hedi Slimane. Fans are excited to see where this association will take him in the future, and if he will ever be officially announced as a CELINE ambassador.
